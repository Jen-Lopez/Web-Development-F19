Bucket-list Movies

A Dog's Journey

Release Date: May 17, 2019

I watched the first installment, A Dog's Purpose, and absolutely loved it. It shows the love and strong bond there is between humans and animals-a love like no other.

Black Widow

Release Date: May 1, 2020

I recently started getting into MCU films. The only movies I've watched to date are the last two installments of the Avengers and Guardian's of the Galaxy. Although The Avengers series ended, Natasha Romanoff is definitely something I'm looking forward to this Spring.

Detective Pikachu

Release Date: May 10, 2019

Because you can't go wrong with Ryan Reynolds as anything, especially Pikachu.

Joker

Release Date: October 4, 2019

I'm excited to watch this film very soon. Heath Ledger is definitely one Joker to beat. But from the trailer, Joaquin Phoenix looks terrifyingly good.

MCU Avengers

Release Date(s): 2012-2019

Like I mentioned earlier, I've only watched the last two installments of The Avengers. It took me a while to get onboard with the MCU fandom but I'm trying!

Mulan

Release Date: March 27, 2020

I did not watch the orignal animated film until about three months ago. Now, I can't stop watching it. I hope Mushu doesn't get cut off from the film.

Spiderman: Far From Home

Release Date: June 26, 2019

I haven't watched the first film starring Tom Holland as Peter Parker either. I think that Tobey Maguire served his role as Spiderman but I'm looking forward to actually watching Tom's version.
Fun Fact: The first film, Spiderman: Homecoming, was actually filmed in my neighborhood!

Toy Story 4

Release Date: June 21, 2019

An end to a great film series. Toy story has played a big role in the lives of people who grew up watching it (like myself). The previous films were adventurous alike inspirational, and instilled valuable life lessons. It'll be a bittersweet goodbye.
